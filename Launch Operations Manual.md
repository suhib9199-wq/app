# Launch Operations Manual

## Pre-Launch
- Verify domain and DNS.
- Enable SSL.
- Upload production build.
- Connect database.
- Test forms and CRM.
- Confirm analytics.
- Backup staging.

## Launch Day
- Deploy to production.
- Clear cache.
- Re-test all critical flows.
- Monitor error logs.
- Confirm WhatsApp/Email buttons.

## Post Launch
- Daily monitoring first week.
- Weekly performance review.
- Lead tracking.
- Content updates.
- Security checks.
