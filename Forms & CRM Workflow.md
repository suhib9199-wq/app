# Forms & CRM Workflow

## Forms
- Contact Form
- Request Quote Form
- Import/Export Inquiry
- Real Estate Inquiry
- Appointment Booking

## Workflow
1. Visitor submits form.
2. Data validates.
3. Lead created in CRM.
4. Notification sent.
5. Staff assigned.
6. Follow-up recorded.
7. Status updated.
8. Report generated.
